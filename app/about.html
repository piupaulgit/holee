About

The Manzoni concept like all good things is a simple one. From our base in Sydney Australia we've been delighting the Australian market with fashion leather accessories for the last 25 years.

And at Manzoni its all about leather. However........ We're also about style! Combining high end fashion trends with popular street fashion. Blurring the boundaries between the stylish and the practical all designed for today's modern lifestyle and up to date trends.

Last but certainly not least, we're all about service, about making your Manzoni shopping experience a pleasure from beginning to end and taking the hassle out of your online experience.
